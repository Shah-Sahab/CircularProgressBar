Welcome to the CircularProgressBar/LimitIndicator wiki!

Circular Progress Bar (a.k.a Limit Indicator) as the name describes indicates the limit of something being used. For example out of 20 gallons gas, one used 10 gallons of it. Or Your total value was 100 and you have used 80. To show the progress of such usage, I have created the limit indicator. This is an Android Library Project, therefore it can only be used in Android. This widget will be used to show the progress of that particular used and unused area.
